Pronoun Slideshow

Subjective pronouns act as the subject of a sentence. Examples: I, You, He, She, They.

Objective pronouns act as the object of a sentence. Examples: Me, You, Him, Her, Them.

Possessive pronouns show ownership. Examples: Mine, Yours, His, Hers, Theirs.

Reflexive pronouns reflect back on the subject. Examples: Myself, Yourself, Himself, Herself.

Demonstrative pronouns point to something specific. Examples: This, That, These, Those.
